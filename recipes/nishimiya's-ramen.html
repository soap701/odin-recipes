Nishimiya's Seafood Cup Ramen

Description

Kyoto Jujutsu High might suck, but not their recipes! Try Momo Nishimiya's signature dish today! It'll make anyone forget their late-night hangry grudges!

  • Level: Easy
  • Yields: 1 serving
  • Prep: 5 min
  • Cook: 5 min
  • Total: 10 min

Ingredients

  • Seafood cup ramen
  • Hot chili peppers
  • Milk
  • Hot water (since we're using milk, make sure the water's boiling hot)

Steps

  1. Chop up hot chili peppers using scissors into the seafood cup ramen.
  2. Fill the container half way with milk.
  3. Add hot water up to the line inside.
