Itadori's Meatballs

Description

Yuji Itadori's (2003-2018) legacy: his meatballs! Super delicious and easy as "even Fushiguro could make them", they're yours to try right now!

  • Level: Easy
  • Yields: 2-5 servings
  • Prep: 25 min
  • Cook: 5 min
  • Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Finely chopped green onions
  • Twice as much ginger paste
  • Egg yolk (you can use the whites too, if it's too much hassle)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix together well and knead it.
  3. Shape meatballs with spoons and toss them into the pot.
